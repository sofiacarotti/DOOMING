DOOMING

A multidisciplinary artistic research project.

Live performance. Dance film. Photographic research. Theoretical embodiment study.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

DOOMING is a multimedia artistic project exploring digital overstimulation, algorithmic conditioning, and the fragmentation of perception under platform capitalism.

The project includes:

  • Live dance performance
  • Dance film production
  • Photographic shooting
  • Embodied research and training methodology

ARTISTIC RESEARCH

The core of DOOMING lies in a physical training system developed by [Your Name] and Marilena.

Instant composition by accumulation.

The method recreates in the body:

  • Overstimulation
  • Loss of spatial orientation
  • Memory saturation
  • Movement degradation
  • Impulse interruption

Through progressive accumulation of tasks and physical layers, the performer experiences cognitive overload translated into corporeal distortion.
